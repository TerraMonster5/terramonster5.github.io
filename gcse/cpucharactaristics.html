Charactaristics of CPUs

How do different factors affect the speed of a CPU?

Clock Speed - The faster the clock speed the more calculations it can do faster. 1Hz = 1 portion (Fetch, Decode or Execute) of a cycle a second.

Cache Size - The larger the cache size the faster the CPU will be because it can store more frequently used data.

Number of Cores - The more cores a CPU has the more calcutlations it will be able to perform simultaneously.
